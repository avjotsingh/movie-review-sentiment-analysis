IMDB Review Sentiment Prediction

A flask app that lets users submit a movie review and predicts the sentiment of the submitted review as either positive or negative. The navigation bar above lets users navigate between different parts of the app.

The app uses a random forest classifier trained on IMDB movie review dataset to make predictions on the user submitted reviews. For training purpose, the reviews from the dataset have been cleaned by removing HTML tags, special characters, punctutation, and stopwords. All words are converted to lowercase before training the classifier. Additionally, a bag-of-words model is used to convert textual data to a sparse feature matrix for training the classifier. When a user submits a review, the same kind of preprocessing is applied before making a prediction.
